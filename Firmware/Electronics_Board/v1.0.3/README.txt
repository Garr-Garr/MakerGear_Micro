Step 1: Format a MicroSD Card to the "FAT32" or "exFAT" filesystem

Step 2: Copy the "Firmware.bin" file onto the SD card

Step 3: Safely eject the SD card from your computer

Step 4: Insert the firmware SD card into the MicroSD card port on the SKR Mini e3 V2 electronics board

Step 5: Reset or reboot the printer (There is a small white reset button close to the MicroSD card adapter on the board)

Step 6: The TFT LCD screen should automatically connect once the firmware is finished uploading
